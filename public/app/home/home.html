Welcome to Victorian Placements
The Victorian Aboriginal Place Names project aims at creating on online tool/blog to connect the aboriginal people with the present community.
Aboriginal Map
View aboriginal places on an interactive map.Clicking onto a place would bring up a popup containing information about the place.
Place Dictionary
See aboriginal names of places in Victoria.
